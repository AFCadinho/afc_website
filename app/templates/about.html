Adinho

Hi, I’m Adinho! I’ve been passionate about competitive Pokémon since 2010, and over the years, I’ve been fortunate to achieve some fun milestones in the game. Here are a few highlights:

  • Rank 3 on PokeMMO and Pokémon Showdown (Gen 9 OU)—I value efficiency over grinding.
  • Rank 1 on PokeOne and Pokémon Revolution Online.
  • In-game tournament wins and unofficial Showdown tournaments.

I love sharing my journey on YouTube to connect with fellow Pokémon fans. When I’m not battling, I enjoy coding, playing guitar, and watching anime. Thanks for stopping by!

This Website

Welcome to my website! This was initially a project for me to get practice working with databases and tightening up my backend development skills. I've kept the design simple since my focus is mainly on the creation of a solid, functional backend infrastructure.

While this is still an early version, I'm excited to share it with you and to continue improving it in due course. Previously, I posted my content on Patreon, but everything will be here now, absolutely free. I have prioritized security and usability to make sure that anyone who visits will have a safe and seamless experience.

This site also works as a portfolio project where I put into practice and further develop my technical skills. You'll find Pokémon teams, strategies, and other resources to facilitate connection, collaboration, and improvement in the competitive scene. Thank you for visiting! I'm constantly working on updates, so check back often for new features and content. Let's grow and enjoy the journey together!
